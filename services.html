Global Employment

Explore our GEO Services

The number of European and American organizations that are recruiting in Asia, including Australia and New Zealand, has been steadily growing over the past decade. Every major organization has at least one recruiter in Asia and many smaller organizations have at least engaged a third-party recruitment firm or have outsourced their recruitment to a recruitment process outsourcing firm.

A GEO is an international solution for hiring abroad, without having a branch or subsidiary in the foreign location. This streamlines the hiring process and instead of sending employees on international assignment as in the past, a company can recruit and hire a skilled, local resident to fill the role.

While some management roles can still come from the home office, as an employer of record, sometimes known as a global PEO, we handle all payroll, benefits, tax and compliance requirements for your new global workers, so you can focus on their contributions to your organization, not on their risk.

Geo Services

Arranges work permits and visas for expat employees

When a client is assigning employees from home, the GEO can sponsor work permits through the employer of record as the legal employer.

Runs a local payroll through the employer of record

By running a local payroll, the employee receives a payslip just like any other employee, along with required withholdings and contributions.

Ensures that the employment contract meets local standards

In addition to having acute knowledge of local laws and cultures, we offer contracts in local languages, salaries in local currencies, and support in your local time zone.

A longer name than just “Legal” would look better

Advises the client on relevant employment laws such as notice, severance, termination and statutory leave.

Human resources management system (HRIS)

You and your employee gain access to our secure HRIS compliant platform to manage employee’s record, on-boarding, off-boarding, time off and payroll from any device.
